Research Philosophy

My work has focused on a single enduring question: how can intelligent systems combine perception, memory, reasoning, and scientific knowledge to support human decision making?

From multimedia retrieval to healthcare AI and multimodal foundation models, the goal has remained consistent: developing AI systems that augment human expertise in solving complex real-world problems.
